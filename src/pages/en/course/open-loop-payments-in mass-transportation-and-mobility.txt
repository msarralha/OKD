<h1>Open-Loop Payments in Transit Systems</h1>

<p>Open-loop payments allow passengers to use their bank-issued EMV cards or digital wallets to access public transportation, eliminating the need for dedicated transit cards. This model is gaining traction globally for its flexibility, scalability, and customer convenience.</p>

  <li>What is an open-loop payment system?</li>
  <li>How EMV-based fare collection differs from legacy systems</li>
  <li>System architecture, including acquirers, PSPs, aggregators, and validators</li>
  <li>Implementation models: Aggregated vs. Instant Fare Collection</li>
  <li>Benefits to municipalities, operators, and riders</li>
</ul>

<p>This training is tailored for transit agencies, consultants, solution providers, and smart city professionals seeking to modernize urban mobility with inclusive, interoperable fare collection systems.</p>
