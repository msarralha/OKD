<h1>EMV & Mobility for Executives and Leaders</h1>

<p>This executive training offers a high-level understanding of EMV and open-loop payments, focusing on strategic, financial, and governance aspects relevant to decision-makers in mobility and urban infrastructure.</p>

<h2>Executive Insights</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Why cities are moving toward open payment ecosystems</li>
  <li>Cost-benefit analysis vs. traditional systems</li>
  <li>Institutional models: Public-Private Partnerships, SaaS, white label</li>
  <li>Data-driven decision making and mobility-as-a-service (MaaS)</li>
  <li>Overcoming resistance from incumbent AFC providers</li>
</ul>

<p>Designed for public authorities such as mayors, mobility secretaries and executives such as c-level executives, board members, and institutional partners evaluating the future of transportation payments in their region.</p>
